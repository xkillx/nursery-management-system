# Booking Patterns Captured Ahead of Billing Integration

Booking patterns (a child's planned weekly attendance, composed of site-scoped session types) are introduced as capture-only data with effective-dated versioning. The shipped billing model continues to derive monthly invoice billable minutes from attendance actuals (check-in/check-out), not from planned schedules, so booking patterns do not drive invoicing, funding, daily-register, or occupancy in this release; those consumers are deferred to later work. This avoids reversing the audit-relevant "Invoice Source of Truth = attendance actuals" decision, lets the planned-attendance data model and management UX stabilize before being wired into billing/funding, and keeps attendance Sign In/Out the sole source of billing actuals. The trade-off is that booking patterns have no immediate financial effect, so their early value is operational (planning and future register generation) rather than revenue-affecting.
